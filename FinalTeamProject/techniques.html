Techniques

Design Your Website for Your Target Audience
Visitors to your website are looking for something specific. It is important to provide the content that allows them to quickly find what they are looking for (Enchanting Marketing, 2021).
Chunk Your Content
Each paragraph should cover only one topic (UMB, 2021). Coding information in chunks allows visitors to your website to locate the information pertinent to them.
Be Concise
Short, descriptive paragraphs are quick and easy to read (Bernstein, 2002). Omit unnecessary words and fluff (Price, 2004). A well-written paragraph allows scanners (see Write for Scanners below) to easily find the information they are looking for.
Write for Scanners
Most visitors to websites scan the information on the webpage (Enchanting Marketing, 2021)(Price, 2004). People are busy. They don't always have time to read lengthy sentences or paragraphs. Keep your content brief.
Write Meaningful Headers
Using short and direct headers allow readers to easily navigate content. The header should describe the content in each paragraph (UMB, 2021). Scanners often use headers to quickly find information pertinent to them.
