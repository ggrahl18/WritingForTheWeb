Writing for the Web - At Its Best

Web designers must put a great deal of thought into writing for the Web. There are many considerations to take into account. A website should load quickly, be attractive and easy-to-use, and hold the focus of visitors to the website.

We will focus on five techniques we think are the most important. The five techniques overlap and complement each other. A good website will ideally include all five techniques.
